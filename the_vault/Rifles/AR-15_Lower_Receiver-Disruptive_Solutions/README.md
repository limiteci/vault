# Disruptive Solutions AR-15 Lower Receiver 

#### Description

Ive finally had the time to finish up and upload my AR-15 Lower Receiver/stock set.  Included in the release are both STL and IGS files of the project.

This model has not been tested so anyone who does decide to print this out may have to make small adjustments to the printed parts. All Files are provided as is and free to modify, Duplicate and share. For help/support and other 3D printed weapons Visit [the forum](http://forums.defcad.com/showthread.php?848-Disruptive-Solutions-AR-15-Release-version&p=5004).
